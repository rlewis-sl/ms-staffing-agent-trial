# Requirements for Staffing Agent

## Data Access

- Consultant profiles in SharePoint (Word format)
- Staff metadata (Kimble report)
- Staff availability (Kimble report)
- Consultant leave (Kimble report)
- Long term absence assignments (Kimble report)
- Consultant skills (Kimble report)
- Project Hub descriptions (Confluence pages)

## Query Types

### Consultants and teams for projects

- What consultant or team could meet a staffing request for a project from [start] for [duration] requiring [number] consultants with [seniority-levels] using [technology] for [client] in [domain/industry] and fulfilling [special-requirements]

### Clients, projects and assignments

- Which consultants are working for [client]?
- What projects do we have at [client]?
- Who is the engagement lead / delivery principal on [project]?
- List the current assignments at [client]
- When do our projects at [client] end?
- What role does [consultant] have on [project]?
- Which clients are we currently working with?
- How many consultants are assigned to [client]?
- Show me the team on [project]

### Consultant availability

- Is a specific consultant available?
- When will a consultant be available?
- What assignments does a consultant have?
- Who is available on a specific date?
- Which consultants are available in a particular location?
- Are there any free consultants?

### Bench time or Bench status

- Who is currently on the bench?
- What's the bench status?
- Which consultants are unassigned?
- When was a consultant last on the bench?
- How long has a consultant been on the bench?
- Who has upcoming bench time?
- Check bench status

### Consultant information

- Which office/location does a consultant work in?
- When did a consultant join the company?
- What is a consultant's role?
- Has a consultant left the company?
- How long has a consultant been with the company?
- List all consultants at the company
- Show all current employees
- How many consultants work at the company?

### Session history [optional]

- How many chats do I have?
- Summarise my chats
- Show my recent chats
- Show deleted chats
- Restore that chat
- Which chats have ratings?
- Find a chat about [topic]
